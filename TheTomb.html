The Tomb

Ice Staff

How to build the wonder weapon, the ice staff, on this map.

Step 1

Kill the shock mimic on round 8 and pick up the monocle it drops

Step 2

  1. Open the door to nowhere
  2. The objective is to get the markings near the hunter drawing, if the crystal on the ceiling isn’t lit up shoot the nearby crystals on the ceiling until it is next to the hunter photo.
  1. Do the same but with the bull mural

Step 3

For this step you need to shoot the symbols in order

  1. Bull
  1. Hunter

After you do the lockdown steps pick up the parts and build them in the crazy place to get the staff.

Upgrading the staff

This is the steps to upgrading the ice staff.

  1. Shoot three of the floating crystals with the ice staff. If done correctly Archibald will start talking about flames being extinguished.
    Note: There are only three crystals on the map at once but they’re in certain positions within a group of positions for each one of the three possible crystals on the map.

  2. Shoot the three symbols on the rocks floating in the sky in the crazy place. Note the symbols down.

  3. One of the doors will close in the crazy place. Go to its position in the outside world and then shoot the three symbols you shot from the rocks.

  4. Go through the door. Hold f on the purple ball and escort the purple ball through the crazy place. Make sure the whole team is in the crazy place or else it fails automatically.

  5. Hold f on the upgrade hole and it will upgrade the staff.
